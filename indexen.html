THE METAL HORDE

BIOGRAPHY

From the depths of Évora Cathedral, a project emerged in the year of our lord 2021, Danger Machine... the return of Street Metal!

Formed by the vocalist/bassist of the same name and drummer War “Rex” Rhino, the first demo tape was forged in a university dorm room smelling of CHEAP TOBACCO and BAGGED WINE – “Metallick Mercenaries”, originally released independently on K7 in 2022 (under a different name) and later released on CD by Maledict Records in 2024.

The proposal of this demo was to go against the production standards of current metal in Portugal, rejecting commercial conceptions and creating a sound that wanders through Black’n’Roll, Traditional Heavy Metal, Speed Metal and Thrash Metal – offending unprepared ears, dividing opinions, but leaving no soul indifferent… the musical equivalent of “tired horse soups”.

Although initially conceived as a studio project, refusing live performances in its first years, in 2023 the Count of Lucefecit and his infernal captaincy decided to unleash horse doses of testosterone on stage. In 2024 they recorded and released, again through Maledict Records, the live EP “Facadas, Sangue & Rock'n'Roll” – continuing the demonstration of RAW FORCE and IGNORANCE this time on stages, with decibels adding another dimension to the riffs.

In 2025 the project played iconic festivals of the Portuguese Metal and Punk scene such as NADA Music Fest (Beja), Heavy Duty Fest (Lisbon) and Fatela Sónica (Fundão), where they shared the bill with names like Pentagram (USA), Sortilège (FR), Savage Grace (USA) and Suicidal Angels (GR). In 2026/2027 the schedule promises appearances at others such as Alvalade Arise (Alvalade - Alentejo) and HARDMETAL Fest (Mangualde).
